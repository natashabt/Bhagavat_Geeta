What is nature?
prakriti is god's agent working under the direction of god there are three modes of nature:
  1. 1.mode of goodness
  2. 2.mode of passion
  3. 3.mode of ignorance
when the person is in mode of goodness he/she is happy and satisfied. when the person is in the mode of passion he tries to accuire things and when the person is in mode of ignorance he does not know what to do.
